Choice 2 - Part 1: Invest in Clothing Company

He met with the owner of the clothing company and discussions began. They both set conditions for what they wanted. The discussions lasted up to three weeks as a lot of compromising was to be done.

Mr Duck realzied the amount of work he would have to put in. He agreed to the terms and became partners with the owner of the clothing company. He invested a lot of money, and used his network to get the business to grow faster. The business was running well, but he felt like there was something missing.

Mr Duck found that the feeling was coming from the fact that he had to compromise a lot. He did not like the feeling, so he decided to pull out his investments and he left the company.
