Choice 2 - Part 2: Start own Clothing Business

He decided to start up his own Clothing company. The company was to be called Mr Duck's Clothes. Mr Duck built factories, and gave contracts to shipping companies.

The start-up of the company was done. He had his clothing network set up and business was progressing well. A lot of the big shops started selling his clothes. He monitored the business for three months, and saw that it was doing well and no longer needed supervision. He appointed someone to be the director of the sub-company and took a break from the clothing company.
